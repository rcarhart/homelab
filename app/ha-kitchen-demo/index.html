Creamy Tuscan Chicken Pasta

Ingredients on the left, directions on the right, optimized for kitchen readability.

Ingredients
2 chicken breasts
12 oz pasta
1 cup heavy cream
3 cloves garlic
1/2 cup parmesan
Fresh basil
Sun-dried tomatoes
Directions
1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente, then reserve a little pasta water.
2. Season chicken, sear until golden, then rest and slice.
3. Sauté garlic and sun-dried tomatoes, then stir in cream and parmesan.
4. Add pasta and chicken back in, loosen with pasta water if needed.
5. Finish with basil, plate, and serve.
