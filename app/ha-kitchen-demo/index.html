Creamy Tuscan Chicken Pasta

Designed for real kitchen use: clear ingredients, readable steps, and controls large enough for a wall tablet while cooking.

Ingredients

Checklist

7 items
2 chicken breasts
12 oz pasta
1 cup heavy cream
3 garlic cloves
1/2 cup parmesan
Fresh basil
Sun-dried tomatoes
Directions

Step-by-step

Readable cooking flow
1. Boil the pasta in salted water until al dente. Reserve a little pasta water before draining.
2. Season the chicken, sear until golden, and let it rest before slicing.
3. Sauté garlic and sun-dried tomatoes, then add cream and parmesan to build the sauce.
4. Add sliced chicken and pasta back into the pan. Loosen with pasta water if needed.
5. Finish with basil, taste for seasoning, and serve warm.
